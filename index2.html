p5 Game Project: Space Invaders

Rules/Tips:

  • You can move around by using the arrow keys
  • Click the space bar to shoot lasers
  • If a ship touches you, you lose the game, you only get one chance per round
  • To win the game, destory all the ships
  • Have fun and enjoy the game!!!
